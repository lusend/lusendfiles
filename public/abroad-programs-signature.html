Signature Programs Experiencing a different culture in the context of a biblical Christian community.
LU Send Study Abroad Signature Program: Participating in this unique opportunity allows students to use their Liberty scholarships and take special Liberty courses while studying abroad for a semester or summer. Students have the opportunity to dive deeper into their host culture through academic or service internships.
  • Quito, Ecuador | Living and Learning International
  • Rome, Italy | Rome With Purpose
  • Belfast, Northern Ireland | Irish Studies
  • Lilongwe, Malawi Africa | African Bible Colleges
NOTE: Students can use their Liberty awarded scholarships for Signature Programs for program cost. For more information on Signature Program specifics please see the Policies & Guidelines and the FAQs.
